About

Crowdfunding can be a quick and easy way to meet unanticipated, pressing needs. Today, anyone with a smartphone can participate in making a difference with great ease. More and more people are now raising funds online to tackle emergencies more efficiently. Increasing digital access and the convenience of online payments are driving more and more Indians to take the digital route to mobilize greater support for urgent needs on time.

Unity is an online crowdfunding platform that enables anyone across India to raise funds for small businesses, with great ease. COVID-19 has changed our lifestyle, many lost jobs but many of them are also investing in their start-ups. They need money for the growth of their business.

Unity addresses the many challenges of giving (such as difficulty in discovering the right causes, trust deficit, and overall donor apathy). They share stories of (pre-qualified) individuals and groups with a network of its trusted community and partners, who either specialize in providing and managing microloans to their local communities or can connect with the cause to help with a donation.
